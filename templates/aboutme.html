About Me

Hi! I’m a Chartered Accountant by training, but over the years I’ve found my real interest lies in using data and technology to solve problems and transform how people work. I work with tools like SQL, Power BI, Alteryx, and Excel, and I’m currently diving into Python to grow further. I’m especially passionate about making work processes smarter, clearer, and more human through data and thoughtful design.
